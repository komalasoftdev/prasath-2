Free Fire, a popular battle royale game, was published by Garena and developed by 111 Dots Studio. It was initially released in December 2017 for Android and iOS platforms, according to Wikipedia and Fandom. The game is known for its fast-paced, 10-minute matches where 50 players compete to be the last one standing.
